# Knowledge Representation

Shared Reasoning separates information according to purpose.

-   Protocol: how collaboration proceeds.
-   Rationale: why the methodology exists.
-   Project-State: current accepted understanding.
-   Session-Log: chronological reasoning history.
-   Domain Knowledge Capsules: compact conceptual generators that
    restore reasoning state.

Normalized artefacts are maintained independently.

For transport and onboarding they may be serialized into a single
capsule without losing their conceptual identities.
